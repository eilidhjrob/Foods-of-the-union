Haggis, neeps and tatties

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Good quality haggis 750g -800g
Potatoes 800g
Swede 800g
milk and butter for mashing

Step 1: Cook the haggis as per the manufacturerâ€™s guidelines. Most will recommend that you remove the outer packaging, wrap the haggis in foil and place in a dish of water in the oven however it is also suitable to be microwaved.

Step 2: Peel and dice the potatoes and swede and cook separately until both are soft. Mash the potatoes with milk, butter and seasoning. Do the same with the swede but without the milk.

Step 3: Serve the haggis on a plate alongside the side dishes and have the whisky sauce on the side, to give people the option of pouring over their plate.
